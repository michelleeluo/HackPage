What is HACK about?


Hackathons are a growing trend and an important part of Computer Science education. However, with this growth, comes a growth in various resources, scattered information and opportunities for students. HACK aims to eliminate confusion and amalgamate all potential hackathons into one hub.

Here, you will be able to access and apply to all kinds of hackathons around you, and interact with the evergrowing community of hackers. Additionally, we aim to provide an easy way for organizations to register their hackathons on the website and accept applications.
